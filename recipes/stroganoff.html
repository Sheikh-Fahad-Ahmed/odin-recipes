Chicken Stroganoff

Description

For this chicken stroganoff recipe, I wanted something like the traditional beef stroganoff and threw this one together, writing down everything as I went so I wouldn't forget. I served it with noodles, but rice would be great too. For a complete meal, make asparagus for a vegetable side and a green or fruit salad. Nutmeg seems to be the secret ingredient of many stroganoff recipes.
